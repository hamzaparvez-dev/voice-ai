TOKYO BAY INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL (TBIS)
Campus Facilities & Extracurricular Activities
=================================================

CAMPUS OVERVIEW
---------------
Location: Minato Ward, Tokyo Bay Area
Campus Size: 15,000 square meters
Built: 2018 (purpose-built international school facility)
Capacity: 800 students

ACADEMIC FACILITIES
-------------------
- 45 classrooms with smart board technology
- 3 Science laboratories (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
- 2 Computer labs with 30 stations each
- Design Technology workshop
- 15,000-volume library with digital resources
- 200-seat auditorium
- Multiple collaborative learning spaces

ARTS FACILITIES
---------------
- 500-seat performing arts theater
- Music rooms (band, orchestra, choir)
- Individual practice rooms (12)
- Visual arts studios (2D and 3D)
- Photography darkroom
- Dance studio

SPORTS FACILITIES
-----------------
- Full-size gymnasium (basketball, volleyball)
- 25-meter indoor swimming pool
- Outdoor soccer/field hockey pitch
- Tennis courts (2)
- Fitness center
- Climbing wall
- Running track

OUTDOOR SPACES
--------------
- Playground (PYP)
- Outdoor learning garden
- Covered lunch area
- Green spaces for recreation

SCHOOL SERVICES
