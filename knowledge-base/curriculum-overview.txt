TOKYO BAY INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL (TBIS)
Curriculum Overview
=====================

INTERNATIONAL BACCALAUREATE (IB) PROGRAMMES
--------------------------------------------
TBIS is an authorized IB World School offering three IB programmes:

1. PRIMARY YEARS PROGRAMME (PYP) - Ages 3-11 (Preschool to Grade 5)
   - Inquiry-based learning approach
   - Transdisciplinary themes connecting subjects
   - Focus on developing the whole child
   - Languages: English (primary), Japanese, Mandarin (optional)
   - Core subjects: Language, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Arts, PE

2. MIDDLE YEARS PROGRAMME (MYP) - Ages 11-16 (Grades 6-10)
   - Eight subject groups with interdisciplinary connections
   - Personal Project in Grade 10
   - Service learning component
   - Subject groups: Language & Literature, Language Acquisition, Individuals & Societies, Sciences, Mathematics, Arts, Design, Physical & Health Education

3. DIPLOMA PROGRAMME (DP) - Ages 16-19 (Grades 11-12)
   - Rigorous pre-university curriculum
   - Six subject groups plus Core (TOK, EE, CAS)
   - Recognized by universities worldwide
   - Average IB score: 34/45 (above world average)

TEACHING APPROACH
-----------------
- Student-centered inquiry learning
- Small class sizes (average 18 students)
- 1:8 teacher-student ratio
- Differentiated instruction for diverse learners
- Technology integration (1:1 iPad Grades 1-5, 1:1 laptop Grades 6-12)

LANGUAGES
---------
- Primary language of instruction: English
- Japanese: Required from Grade 1
- Additional languages: Mandarin, Spanish, French (electives Grades 6+)
- EAL support available

ASSESSMENT
----------
- Formative and summative assessments
- IB external examinations (MYP eAssessment, DP exams)
- Progress reports: 3 times per year
- Parent-teacher conferences: 2 times per year

ACCREDITATIONS
--------------
- International Baccalaureate (IB) World School
- Council of International Schools (CIS)
- Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC)

UNIVERSITY PLACEMENT
--------------------
Recent graduates accepted to:
- University of Tokyo, Waseda, Keio (Japan)
- UCLA, NYU, University of Michigan (USA)
- University of Toronto, UBC (Canada)
- University of Melbourne, ANU (Australia)
- Imperial College London, UCL (UK)

Average DP score: 34/45
