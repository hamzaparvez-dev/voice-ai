TOKYO BAY INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL (TBIS)
Fee Structure 2025-2026
========================

TUITION FEES (Annual)
---------------------
Preschool (Half Day):     ¥1,800,000
Preschool (Full Day):     ¥2,200,000
Kindergarten:             ¥2,400,000
Grades 1-5 (PYP):         ¥2,800,000
Grades 6-10 (MYP):        ¥3,200,000
Grades 11-12 (DP):        ¥3,500,000

ONE-TIME FEES
-------------
Application Fee:          ¥50,000 (non-refundable)
Enrollment/Registration:  ¥300,000 (one-time, non-refundable)
Capital Development Fee:  ¥500,000 (one-time, refundable upon graduation/withdrawal with 6 months notice)

ANNUAL FEES
-----------
Technology Fee:           ¥80,000 (Grades 1-12)
Activity Fee:             ¥120,000 (includes field trips, events)
Bus Transportation:       ¥480,000 (optional, door-to-door service)

PAYMENT OPTIONS
---------------
1. Annual Payment (due by July 31): 2% discount on tuition
2. Semester Payment (2 installments): Due July 31 and January 15
3. Monthly Payment (10 installments): Available through approved payment plan

Payment Methods:
- Bank transfer (domestic and international)
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX)
- Company sponsorship billing available

SIBLING DISCOUNT
----------------
- Second child: 10% tuition discount
- Third child: 15% tuition discount
- Fourth+ child: 20% tuition discount

SCHOLARSHIPS & FINANCIAL AID
----------------------------
Merit Scholarships:
- Academic Excellence: Up to 25% tuition (Grades 9-12)
- Arts/Music Excellence: Up to 20% tuition
- Sports Excellence: Up to 15% tuition

Need-Based Financial Aid:
- Available for qualifying families
- Application required by March 1
- Confidential review process
- Awards range from 10-50% tuition assistance

CORPORATE SPONSORSHIP
---------------------
Many expatriate families receive education allowances from employers. TBIS provides:
- Direct billing to corporate sponsors
- Detailed invoices for reimbursement
- Letters for tax purposes

REFUND POLICY
-------------
- Withdrawal before August 1: 75% tuition refund
- Withdrawal August-September: 50% tuition refund
- Withdrawal after October 1: No refund
- Capital Development Fee: Refundable with 6 months written notice

CONTACT

Finance Office: finance@tbis.jp
