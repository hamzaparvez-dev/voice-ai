TOKYO BAY INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL (TBIS)
Admissions FAQ
================

GENERAL ADMISSIONS INFORMATION
--------------------------------
Tokyo Bay International School (TBIS) offers rolling admissions throughout the academic year, subject to space availability in each grade level.

Application Process:
1. Submit online inquiry form at www.tbis.jp/admissions
2. Schedule a campus tour (virtual or in-person)
3. Submit completed application with required documents
4. Student assessment (age-appropriate)
5. Family interview with Admissions Director
6. Admission decision within 2 weeks of assessment

Required Documents:
- Completed application form
- Previous school records (last 2 years)
- Teacher recommendation letters (2)
- Student passport copy
- Parent/guardian ID
- Immunization records
- Recent photograph

Application Fee: ¥50,000 (non-refundable, due with application)

Age Requirements (as of September 1):
- Preschool: Age 3-4
- Kindergarten: Age 5
- Grade 1: Age 6
- Grade 2-12: Age appropriate for grade level

KEY DATES (2025-2026 Academic Year)
------------------------------------
- Application Opens: Year-round (rolling)
- Priority Deadline for August Start: March 31, 2025
- Assessment Period: April - July 2025
- New Student Orientation: August 20, 2025
- First Day of School: August 25, 2025
- Mid-Year Entry: January 2026 (applications due by November 15, 2025)

ENGLISH LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------
- Preschool/Kindergarten: No English proficiency required
- Grade 1-5: Basic English communication skills preferred
- Grade 6-12: English proficiency assessment required (WIDA or equivalent)
- EAL (English as Additional Language) support available for all grades

JAPANESE LANGUAGE
-----------------
Japanese language instruction is part of the curriculum for all students. No prior Japanese knowledge required for admission.

TRANSFER STUDENTS
-----------------
Transfer students welcome at all grade levels. Credit evaluation for MYP and DP students conducted upon enrollment.

WAITLIST POLICY
---------------
When a grade is full, applicants are placed on a waitlist. Waitlist is active for one academic year. No waitlist fee.
