ICMG L&D — ENROLLMENT PROCESS
==============================

HOW TO ENROLL
1. Log in to the ICMG Learning Portal (learning.icmg-portfolio.example.com)
2. Browse course catalog or search by category
3. Click "Enroll" (申し込む) on the desired course
4. Manager approval is required for most programmes (auto-routed)
5. Receive confirmation email within 24 hours

MANAGER APPROVAL
- Standard courses: approval within 3 business days
- Leadership programmes: nomination by HR or direct manager required
- Compliance courses: auto-enrolled, no approval needed
- If approval is delayed, contact your L&D coordinator

PREREQUISITES
- New Manager Essentials: minimum 6 months in current role
- Executive Leadership Forum: director level or above, HR nomination
- PMP Prep: 3+ years project experience recommended
- Compliance courses: no prerequisites

WAITLIST POLICY
- If a course is full, you may join the waitlist
- Waitlisted employees notified 5 business days before session
- Priority given to employees with pending compliance deadlines

CANCELLATION POLICY
- Cancel at least 5 business days before session start — no penalty
- Late cancellation (within 5 days): manager notification required
- No-show: recorded in training history; repeated no-shows may affect future enrollment

SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS
- Contact L&D desk for accessibility needs or language support
- Recorded sessions available for most online courses within 30 days
