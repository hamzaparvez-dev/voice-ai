ICMG L&D — COMPLIANCE TRAINING REQUIREMENTS
============================================

MANDATORY ANNUAL TRAINING (ALL EMPLOYEES)
-----------------------------------------
1. Workplace Harassment Prevention (ハラスメント防止研修)
   - Duration: 90 minutes
   - Format: Online (self-paced or live)
   - Deadline: September 30 each fiscal year
   - Renewal: Annual

2. Information Security Awareness (情報セキュリティ研修)
   - Duration: 60 minutes
   - Format: Online, self-paced
   - Deadline: August 31 each fiscal year
   - Renewal: Annual

3. APPI / Data Privacy (個人情報保護/APPI研修)
   - Duration: 90 minutes
   - Format: Online live session or recorded
   - Deadline: October 31 each fiscal year
   - Renewal: Annual
   - Required for all employees handling personal data

MANAGER-LEVEL ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS
-------------------------------------
4. Anti-Bribery & Corruption (贈収賄防止研修)
   - Duration: 60 minutes
   - Audience: Managers and above
   - Renewal: Every 2 years
   - Deadline: Within 60 days of promotion to manager role

NEW HIRE REQUIREMENTS
---------------------
- Complete all mandatory compliance training within 30 days of hire
- ICMG Portfolio Orientation within first 14 days
- Compliance status tracked in Learning Portal dashboard

COMPLETION TRACKING
- Completion recorded automatically in Learning Portal
- Managers receive weekly compliance status reports
- Non-completion after deadline: escalation to HR and direct manager
- Repeated non-compliance may affect performance review

CERTIFICATES
- Digital certificate issued upon completion
- Downloadable from Learning Portal → My Certificates
- Valid for 12 months (annual renewal required)

EXEMPTIONS & EXTENSIONS
- Medical or leave-of-absence extensions: contact HR with documentation
- Role changes mid-year: pro-rated deadlines apply
- Contact L&D coordinator for exemption requests — not handled by AI

REPORTING
- Portfolio-wide compliance dashboard available to ICMG leadership
- Individual completion status: Learning Portal → Compliance Status
