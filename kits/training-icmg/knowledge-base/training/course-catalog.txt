ICMG PORTFOLIO LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
Training Course Catalog
=========================

OVERVIEW
ICMG L&D provides professional development programmes for employees across all portfolio companies. Courses are delivered online, in-person at Tokyo HQ, or hybrid.

LEADERSHIP PROGRAMMES
- New Manager Essentials (新入管理職基礎) — 2 days, quarterly
- Executive Leadership Forum (エグゼクティブリーダーシップ) — 5 days, annual
- Coaching for Performance (パフォーマンスコーチング) — 1 day workshop
- Cross-Cultural Communication (異文化コミュニケーション) — half-day, monthly

COMPLIANCE & GOVERNANCE
- Workplace Harassment Prevention (ハラスメント防止研修) — mandatory, annual, 90 min
- Information Security Awareness (情報セキュリティ研修) — mandatory, annual, 60 min
- APPI / Data Privacy (個人情報保護/APPI) — mandatory, annual, 90 min
- Anti-Bribery & Corruption (贈収賄防止) — mandatory for managers, biennial

ONBOARDING
- ICMG Portfolio Orientation (ICMGグループオリエンテーション) — 1 day, for new hires
- Company Culture & Values (企業文化とバリュー) — 2 hours, first week
- Systems & Tools Introduction (社内システム入門) — 3 hours, self-paced online

TECHNICAL SKILLS
- Data Analytics Fundamentals (データ分析基礎) — 2 days
- AI & Automation for Business (AI・自動化ビジネス活用) — 1 day
- Project Management (PMP Prep) (プロジェクト管理) — 5 days
- Financial Literacy for Non-Finance (非財務担当者の財務基礎) — 1 day

SOFT SKILLS
- Business Japanese Keigo (ビジネス日本語敬語) — 4 sessions, for international staff
- Presentation Skills (プレゼンテーションスキル) — 1 day
- Negotiation Essentials (交渉術基礎) — 1 day

ENROLLMENT NOTES
- Most courses require manager approval
- Leadership programmes have nomination process via HR
- Compliance courses are auto-assigned based on role and hire date
- Contact: ld-support@icmg-portfolio.example.com
